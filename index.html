Your journey into the world of computer engineering is an exciting tech adventure.

Computer Engineering, often abbreviated as CpE, is an academic discipline and professional field that encompasses a wide range of knowledge and skills related to the design, development, and optimization of computer systems. This fascinating field typically involves a comprehensive 4-year educational program where students delve into subjects like digital electronics, computer architecture, software engineering, and programming languages. Through this rigorous curriculum, aspiring computer engineers gain the expertise required to contribute to the ever-evolving world of technology and innovation.

Computer engineering is special because it bridges the gap between hardware and software, enabling the creation and optimization of the very devices and systems that power our modern world. It empowers individuals to design and build the technologies that drive innovation in diverse fields, from artificial intelligence to healthcare. Additionally, computer engineering continually evolves, ensuring professionals in this field remain at the forefront of technological advancements, making it an ever-engaging and rewarding career path.

What we do?

Computer engineering students specialize in the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems and hardware. They study a range of subjects including digital circuits, microprocessors, computer architecture, and embedded systems. These students gain a deep understanding of both hardware and software, enabling them to work on various aspects of computer systems, from designing computer chips to developing software applications that run on them. Computer engineering graduates are well-equipped to pursue careers in areas such as computer hardware engineering, embedded systems development, and software engineering with a strong foundation in the underlying hardware components

What are the long-term implications of what we do?

Computer engineering students play a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology. Their education and experiences equip them with the skills and knowledge needed to drive innovation in fields such as artificial intelligence, robotics, and cybersecurity. The solutions they develop can have far-reaching impacts on industries, society, and daily life, from improving healthcare through medical devices to enhancing global communication networks. Additionally, their ethical considerations and decisions as professionals influence the responsible and sustainable development of technology, ensuring it aligns with societal values and needs. Ultimately, the work of computer engineering students contributes to the ongoing evolution of our digital world.

About the CPE department

Mission

The Department of Computer Engineering declares its role to produce competitive engineers who are knowledgeable in the fundamental areas of digital and computer system design, software development, and modeling of physical systems.

Vision

The Department of Computer Engineering will impact the information age as a national leader in computing, research, and education.

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES

After three to five years from graduation, the Computer Engineering alumni shall:

1. Have pursued advancement towards becoming globally competitive leaders in their chosen field of practice
2. Be a contributor to the development of a progressive society, guided by the UE core values of Excellence, Integrity, Professionalism, Teamwork, Commitment, Transparency, Accountability and Social Responsibility.
